About CVR College

The Cherabuddi Education Society was registered in January 1999 as an NRI-promoted society. The Society aims at creating a state-of-the-art engineering institution in association with leading NRI technology professionals and well-known academicians of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. The Society aims to harness technical excellence with a commitment to the ethos of useful learning.
