About CVR College of Engineering

The Cherabuddi Education Society was established in January 1999 as an NRI-promoted society with the vision of creating a world-class engineering institution.

CVR College of Engineering was founded to provide quality technical education through experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and industry-oriented learning.

The institution collaborates with leading technology professionals and academicians to promote innovation, research, and academic excellence.

CVR College is committed to producing competent engineers with strong ethical values and technical skills to meet global challenges.
